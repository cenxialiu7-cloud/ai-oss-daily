# begin0808/VoxCPM2

基於 OpenBMB VoxCPM2 模型的離線語音合成與聲音複製桌面工作站（支援即時錄音、語音設計與極限複製） An offline desktop workstation GUI for VoxCPM2 voice synthesis, featuring voice design, speaker cloning, and live voice recording.
