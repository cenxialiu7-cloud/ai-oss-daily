# ale2348/trading-bot

A TypeScript versioned Polymarket Trading Bot for **Polymarket 5-minute crypto Up/Down** markets: **BTC, ETH, SOL, and XRP**. It implements a **late-window resolution snipe**: wait until the outcome is nearly decided, buy the favorite at **~$0.98–$0.99**, then hold to resolution for a small payout on each winning cycle.
