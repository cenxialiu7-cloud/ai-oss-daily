# FrancoisRost1/mini-bloomberg-terminal

Unified investment research terminal. Integrates 10 standalone financial engines (LBO, PE screener, factor models, regime detection, TSMOM, options, portfolio optimization, robustness lab, AI research agent) into 68 institutional workspaces. Live-first production stack: Railway + Cloudflare + FMP + Polygon + FRED + Finnhub + Anthropic + SQLite.
