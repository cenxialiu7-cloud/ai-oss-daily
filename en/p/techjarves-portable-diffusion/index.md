# techjarves/Portable-Diffusion

A portable Local AI Image Generator and Stable Diffusion GUI for Windows, Linux, and macOS. Run GGUF & Safetensors models 100% offline with zero setup and auto-detected GPU/NPU acceleration (CUDA, ROCm, Vulkan, Metal, OpenVINO).
